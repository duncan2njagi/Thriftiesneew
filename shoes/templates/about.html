Welcome to Shoes by Thrifties, your number one source for all things Shoe-related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best that Shoes by Thrifties has to offer, with a focus on the latest shoes and whats trending at the moment.

More about us:

Founded in 2004 by Lilian the Great, Shoes by Thrifties has come a long way from its humble beginnings in our first location at Embu hills. When Lilian the Great first started out, her passion for 'Changing the world one shoe at a time' drove her to dedicate her life to crafting the most innovative and cost-friendly shoes, so that Shoes by Thrifties can offer you the best in the business.
